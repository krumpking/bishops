Bishops Mount Centre Private Limited

Bishops Mount Centre Private Limited is a registered company (registration number 255/2020) and is wholly owned by Anglican Diocese of Harare CPCA. It offers services and facilities perfect for conferences, outdoor and indoor events (such as weddings, parties amongst other activities) and teambuilding. Bishops Mount Centre Pvtd opened its doors on the 1st of January 2022 and is located in the serene and relaxing heart of Greendale suburb, Harare, Zimbabwe. In addition, the Centre also provides a conference room that accommodates up to 80 people as well as 10 fully furnished suited bedrooms that can accommodate up to 20 people.

Accommodation

Bishops Mount offers 10 fully furnished rooms available at USD40 single occupancy and USD50 sharing occupancy per night with the following features: High speed WiFi, Entertainment (Netflix, YouTube), tea and coffee making facilities, refrigerator, electronic entry, borehole water and an optional breakfast.

Retreat/Camp Facilities

For groups and persons interested in hosting retreats, we offer a unique package starting at a low price of USD10, both day and night.
