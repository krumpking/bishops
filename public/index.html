Bishops Mount Centre Private Limited is a registered company (registration number 255/2020) and is wholly owned by Anglican Diocese of Harare CPCA. It offers hospitality services facilities for conferences, outdoor and indoor events (such as weddings, parties among others) as well as teambuilding. Bishops Mount Centre Private Limited opened its doors on the 1 st of January 2022 and is located in the serene and relaxing heart of Greendale suburb, Harare, Zimbabwe. The Centre holds a conference room that accommodates up to 80 people as well as 10 fully furnished suited bedrooms that can accommodate up to 20 people.
